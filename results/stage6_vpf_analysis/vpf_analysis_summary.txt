----------------------------------------------------------------------
3. AERODYNAMIC IMPLICATIONS
----------------------------------------------------------------------

Key Findings:

• Optimal incidence varies significantly across flight conditions.
  This variation represents the pitch adjustment that a VPF
  mechanism could apply to maintain peak efficiency.

• Different blade sections (root, mid_span, tip) show different
  optimal angles due to varying Reynolds numbers and local
  flow conditions.

• A variable pitch mechanism allows the fan to operate at
  maximum efficiency across all flight regimes, improving
  overall engine performance and fuel efficiency.

• The analysis uses the SECOND efficiency peak (alpha >= 3°)
  to avoid the laminar separation bubble artifact at low angles,
  ensuring results are representative of real turbomachinery
  operation.
